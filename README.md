📊 Sales Performance Dashboard
🧾 Project Overview

This project presents a Sales Performance Dashboard created using Excel to analyze business performance across different dimensions such as region, manager, product category, and returns.

The dashboard provides clear insights into sales trends, profitability, and operational efficiency, helping stakeholders make data-driven decisions.

🎯 Objectives
Analyze overall sales and profit performance
Identify top-performing regions and managers
Track returned orders
Build an interactive and dynamic dashboard
Visualize KPIs for quick business understanding
🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used
Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables, Charts, Slicers)
Data Cleaning & Transformation
KPI Design & Dashboarding

Orders

🔍 Key Insights
💡 1. Regional Performance
Certain regions contribute significantly higher sales compared to others
Some regions show high sales but low profit, indicating cost inefficiencies
💡 2. Manager Performance
Top-performing managers generate the highest revenue
Performance variation exists — highlighting training or strategy gaps
💡 3. Profitability Analysis
Not all high sales lead to high profit
Some categories/regions operate at low margins or losses
💡 4. Returned Orders
A noticeable percentage of orders are returned
Indicates potential issues in:
Product quality
Delivery
Customer satisfaction
💡 5. Business Opportunities
Focus on high-performing regions for scaling
Improve low-profit areas by cost optimization
Reduce return rates to increase overall efficiency
